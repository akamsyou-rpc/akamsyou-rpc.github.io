aka.ms/yourpc



Phone Link allows you to seamlessly connect your Windows 11 PC to your Android or iPhone. With "aka.ms/yourpc", you can access messages, calls, notifications, photos, and even use your favorite mobile apps right from your computer. Essentially, it aims to improve productivity and enhance user experience by reducing the friction of switching between devices.


Key Features

  • Sync Notifications: Get your phone notifications directly on your PC, so you never miss an important alert.
  • Access Photos: View and transfer photos from your phone to your PC with ease.
  • Send Messages: Send and receive text messages from your PC, making communication more efficient.
  • Make and Receive Calls: Handle calls directly from your PC without needing to pick up your phone.

How to set Up Aka.ms/yourpc?

  1. Check Compatibility: Ensure you have a Windows 10 or Windows 11 device and a compatible Android or iOS smartphone.
  2. Download the App:
    • For Android: Install the "Link to Windows" app from the Google Play Store.
    • For iOS: Use the "Your Phone" app from the Microsoft Store, although the experience may be limited compared to Android.
  3. Link Your Devices:
    • Open the "Your Phone" app on your PC.
    • After launching the app, you will be prompted to visit Aka.ms/yourpc.
    • Follow the on-screen instructions to scan a QR code or enter a code displayed on your PC.
  4. Sign In: Log in with the same Microsoft account on both devices to sync your content.
  5. Grant Permissions: Follow the prompts to allow necessary permissions on your mobile device, such as access to notifications, contacts, and messages.
  6. Enjoy the Integration: Once linked, you can start using the features immediately. Adjust settings as per your preferences to customize your experience.

What you can do with Phone Link?

  • Stay Connected: Check texts, calls, and notifications on your PC without taking your phone out of your pocket.
  • Read and Respond: Read and reply to messages directly from your computer.
  • Make and Take Calls: Use your PC to make and receive calls.
  • View Recent Photos: Access the last 2,000 images from your Android device and easily share them.

Compatibility and Requirements:

  • Phone Link works with Windows 10 May 2019 Update or later.
  • If you’re on Windows 10, search for “Phone Link” in the Start menu or download it directly from the Microsoft Store.

Benefits of Using Aka.ms/yourpc

  • Enhanced Productivity: With easy access to phone notifications, texts, and calls right on your PC, you can stay focused on work without constantly checking your phone.
  • Convenience: The integration of mobile apps into a desktop environment allows for more efficient multitasking and accessibility.
  • Improved Communication: Whether it's responding to messages or making calls, Your Phone helps streamline your communication methods.
  • Streamlined Workflow: Transferring photos and files is a breeze, as you can avoid cumbersome email methods or third-party apps.
  • Cross-Platform Compatibility: The service works well across different platforms, making it a versatile tool for users with varying devices.
